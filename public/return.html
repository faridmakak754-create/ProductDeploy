Return & Refund Policy

Please review our return & refund guidelines carefully before making any payments.

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase.

1. Return Eligibility

  • Products may be returned within 7 days of delivery if:
  • The item is unused
  • It is in original packaging
  • All accessories are included

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Products damaged by the customer
  • Opened or activated software products
  • Items without original packaging

3. Refund Process

Refunds will be processed after product inspection. The amount will be credited within 5-7 working days to the original payment method.

4. Replacement Policy

Defective products may be eligible for replacement subject to stock availability.

5. Important Note

An unboxing video is recommended for claiming damage or missing items.

We aim to provide a smooth and transparent return and refund process for our customers. If you have any concerns regarding your order, please contact our support team, and we will assist you as quickly as possible.
